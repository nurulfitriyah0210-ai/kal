    "# Materi kal\n",
    "# SISTEM PERSAMAAN LINIER (SPL)  \n",
    "## dan Solusinya dengan Operasi Baris Elementer\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 1. Pengertian Sistem Persamaan Linier\n",
    "\n",
    "Sistem Persamaan Linier (SPL) adalah kumpulan dari dua atau lebih persamaan linear dg variabel yg sama.\n",
    "\n",
    "Tujuan Utama: Menentukan nilai variabel yg memenuhi semua persamaan secara bersamaan.\n",
    "\n",
    "## Contoh Umum\n",
    "\n",
    "\\[\n",
    "\\begin{aligned}\n",
    "x + y &= 5 \\\\\n",
    "2x - y &= 1\n",
    "\\end{aligned}\n",
    "\\]\n",
    "\n",
    "Bentuk umum SPL dengan \\( m \\) persamaan dan \\( n \\) variabel:\n",
    "\n",
    "\\[\n",
    "\\begin{aligned}\n",
    "a_{11}x_1 + a_{12}x_2 + \\dots + a_{1n}x_n &= b_1 \\\\\n",
    "a_{21}x_1 + a_{22}x_2 + \\dots + a_{2n}x_n &= b_2 \\\\\n",
    "\\vdots \\\\\n",
    "a_{m1}x_1 + a_{m2}x_2 + \\dots + a_{mn}x_n &= b_m\n",
    "\\end{aligned}\n",
    "\\]\n",
    "\n",
    "dengan:\n",
    "\n",
    "- \\( a_{ij} \\) = koefisien\n",
    "- \\( x_i \\) = variabel\n",
    "- \\( b_i \\) = konstanta\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 2. Bentuk Matriks SPL\n",
    "\n",
    "SPL dapat ditulis dalam bentuk matriks:\n",
    "\n",
    "\\[\n",
    "A X = B\n",
    "\\]\n",
    "\n",
    "dengan:\n",
    "\n",
    "\\[\n",
    "A =\n",
    "\\begin{bmatrix}\n",
    "a_{11} & a_{12} & \\dots & a_{1n} \\\\\n",
    "a_{21} & a_{22} & \\dots & a_{2n} \\\\\n",
    "\\vdots & \\vdots & \\ddots & \\vdots \\\\\n",
    "a_{m1} & a_{m2} & \\dots & a_{mn}\n",
    "\\end{bmatrix}\n",
    "\\quad\n",
    "X =\n",
    "\\begin{bmatrix}\n",
    "x_1 \\\\\n",
    "x_2 \\\\\n",
    "\\vdots \\\\\n",
    "x_n\n",
    "\\end{bmatrix}\n",
    "\\quad\n",
    "B =\n",
    "\\begin{bmatrix}\n",
    "b_1 \\\\\n",
    "b_2 \\\\\n",
    "\\vdots \\\\\n",
    "b_m\n",
    "\\end{bmatrix}\n",
    "\\]\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 3. Operasi Baris Elementer (OBE)\n",
    "\n",
    "Untuk menyelesaikan SPL digunakan metode eliminasi Gauss atau Gauss-Jordan yang berdasarkan Operasi Baris Elementer (OBE).\n",
    "\n",
    "Terdapat 3 jenis OBE:\n",
    "\n",
    "1. Menukar dua baris  \n",
    "   \\[\n",
    "   R_i \\leftrightarrow R_j\n",
    "   \\]\n",
    "\n",
    "2. Mengalikan suatu baris dengan konstanta tak nol  \n",
    "   \\[\n",
    "   R_i \\rightarrow kR_i\n",
    "   \\]\n",
    "\n",
    "3. Menjumlahkan kelipatan suatu baris ke baris lain  \n",
    "   \\[\n",
    "   R_i \\rightarrow R_i + kR_j\n",
    "   \\]\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 4. Matriks Augmented\n",
    "\n",
    "Untuk mempermudah perhitungan, digunakan matriks diperluas (augmented matrix):\n",
    "\n",
    "\\[\n",
    "[A \\mid B]\n",
    "\\]\n",
    "\n",
    "Contoh SPL:\n",
    "\n",
    "\\[\n",
    "\\begin{aligned}\n",
    "2x + y &= 5 \\\\\n",
    "x - y &= 1\n",
    "\\end{aligned}\n",
    "\\]\n",
    "\n",
    "Matriks augmented:\n",
    "\n",
    "\\[\n",
    "\\left[\n",
    "\\begin{array}{cc|c}\n",
    "2 & 1 & 5 \\\\\n",
    "1 & -1 & 1\n",
    "\\end{array}\n",
    "\\right]\n",
    "\\]\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
    "## 5. Penyelesaian dengan Eliminasi Gauss\n",
    "\n",
    "Langkah:\n",
    "\n",
    "1. Bentuk matriks augmented  \n",
    "2. Lakukan OBE hingga menjadi bentuk eselon baris  \n",
    "3. Lakukan substitusi balik  \n",
    "\n",
    "Hasil akhir dapat berupa:\n",
    "\n",
    "- Solusi tunggal\n",
    "- Tak hingga solusi\n",
    "- Tidak ada solusi\n",
    "\n",
    "---\n",
    "\n",
